CINTIQS Analyst Feed

Houthis shoot down Saudi F-15 near Marib, on the evening of 1 April 2020. Social media pictures have shown the wreckage, which cintiqs analysts have confirmed is authentic. There are not pictures of the fate of the pilot. Houthi media have reported that it was shot down with the Fater-1 system. cintiqs comment: This is in line with previous shoot downs of Saudi aircraft
On 4 May 2020, media reported an airstrike that killed a top Quds force commander in Yemen. The US has so far not confirmed these reports.
Cintiqs comment: CINTIQS sources reveal that this commander was integral to Houthi's ability to counter saudi air defences. It is likely Houthsi will be outraged at the US attack and seek to take revenge on Saudi Arabia.
